Food

You can buy normal food, such as milk, vegetables, meat, and anything common in all around the globe countries at Australian grocery stores. (Woolworth, Coles, Aldi, IGA, and etc.)
If you are looking for a specific ingredient to cook something from your childhood, there are specific suburbs where are multiple of cultural shops, where you can buy anything you want
As an example, the Eastwood market is located close to Eastwood station, where you can find Chinese and Korean restaurants, vegetables, ingredients and etc.
Another good example is Auburn for Nepalese and Blacktown/Harris park for Indians (need to have a passport or Photo ID)
