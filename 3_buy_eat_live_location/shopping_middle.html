Shopping

Shopping in the city is always very expensive, however, there are some markets (for example, Haymarket near central station) which helps tourists and other people to find good things to buy with cheap prices.
eBay is an excellent online service of buying anything in Australia, because the usual delivery of items is no longer than 5 working days (Australian sellers)
Gumtree or Facebook market are best places to buy or get for free second-hand items.
