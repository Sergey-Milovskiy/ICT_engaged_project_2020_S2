Introduction
The game start at the scene of fight taxiing, welcome to Australia journey! The game is about how to survive smarter in Australia, which each multiple choice has different marks, the subtotal mark will be added to ending, the marks would indicate the character was having a good/bad life in Australia, also affect the character successful graduate or fail in the subjects. Once step in Australia border, the character will confuse the new environment, which paucity of language skills, none one knows you and expensive goods rate. In this game, each choice might not be the worse option, but they might not that clever in the situations while new travel in Australia. If the gamer does not pick the precise answer, this system will explain what is gonging wrong and why the better choice is suitable here!
