Public Transportation
You can buy Opal single bus tickets from the driver, except on Opal only or PrePay buses.
You can buy Opal single trip tickets from top up and single trip ticket machines at selected metro and train stations, wharves and light rail stops.
Search for your stop, station or wharf to see ticket and other facilities.

Free buses in Sydney
A free city centre shuttle bus (route 555) operates every 10 minutes on a circuit from Central Station to Circular Quay,
along George Street, linking many of Sydney's attractions.
The service operates from 9am to 3.30pm on weekdays (to 9pm on Thursday evenings) and from 9.30am to 6pm on weekends.
