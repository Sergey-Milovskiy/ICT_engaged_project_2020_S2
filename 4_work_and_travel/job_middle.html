International students Legal work

International students in Australia on a valid student visa can work for up to 20 hours per week while school is in session,
and there is no limit on the number of hours an international student can work during recognized school vacations.
The 20-hour-per-week limit does not extend to any work the student is required to undertake as a component of his or her studies or training.
If an international student works more than the Australian restrictions allow, his or her visa may be cancelled
