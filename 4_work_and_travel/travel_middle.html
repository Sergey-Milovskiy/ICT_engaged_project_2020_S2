Festivals during the year

Vivid Festival is a 23-day-long festival of lights and projections that occurs every winter from May to June in Sydney.
You could change the colour of the Harbor Bridge or make a chair light up according to the beat of your heart.

Beaches around Sydney

There are over hundreds of beaches around Sydney, such as famously Bondi Beach, Manly Beach, and Coogee Beach.
While Whale Beach is 30 kilometres away from CBD, here is popular with global surfers, swimmers, and boogie boarders alike.
All above locations we can get there by public transportation with Opal card!
